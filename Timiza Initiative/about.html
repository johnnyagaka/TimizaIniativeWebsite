ABOUT US

"A Candle Loses Nothing By Lighting Another Candle"

Established in November 2015, Timiza Initiative is a Non-Profit Organization that aims a bridging the gap between the rich and poor by steering projects for the underprivileged and vulnerable in society to support them socially, economically, spiritually, sexually and psychologically

The organization firmly believes in the three pillars which are:

Influence, Inspire and Impact
